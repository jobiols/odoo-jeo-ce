Sale Discount Limit

The Sale Discount Limit module prepared by Aktiv Software will help you put a restriction on the sale discount which can only be posted by authoritative person such as Sales Manager and Sales/User. By default, the module has allowed a limit of providing a maximum of 10% discount for Sales Manager and Sales user.

Configurations

  • Go to Sales > Configuration > Sales Discount Limit

Select Sales User Group and Put Discount in Percentage

Having created a user and configured with access rights of "Sales Manager" and "Discount on Lines" they will get the rights to apply a maximum of 15% discount.

Having created a user and configured with access rights of "User : All doc" and "Discount on Lines" they will get the rights to apply a maximum of 10% discount.

Having created a user and configured with access rights of "user: Own doc" and "Discount on Lines" they will get the rights to apply a maximum of 5% discount.
